The Game

Simple rules. Deep strategy. Every move counts.

6×6 Board • 4 Colors • First to Five

Place cards on the board to form a line of 5 matching-color cards in a row, column, or diagonal.

Each card has a value (1–9) and a color. You can overwrite an opponent's card if yours has a higher value — but only on adjacent cells.

4 colors • Values 1–9 • Strategy meets luck.

FAQ

Everything you need to know before your first match.

What if my opponent never joins?

After 30 minutes, you can call claimRefund() on the smart contract to get your full wager back. No risk.

What if the game crashes mid-match?

The same refund mechanism applies. If no result is submitted within the timeout, both players can reclaim their funds.

Is the contract audited?

The contract is open source on GitHub and has passed security hardening tests. You can verify the code yourself.

What's the fee?

5% protocol fee. The winner receives 95% of the total pot (both wagers combined).

How do I get MON?

Bridge ETH to Monad via the official Monad bridge or acquire MON on a supported exchange.
